## 7. DO'S AND DON'TS

### ✅ Always do this:
- Use High Resolution images - Always 2x the banner size minimum
- Test readability - Ensure text is legible on all backgrounds
- For Images - Keep product in focus with proper lighting
- Include Ad tag when required by platform
- Button positioned at 24px from bottom (16px safe zone + 8px padding)
- 38px max flexible spacing between subtext and button absorbs content variations
- For Foreground prodcut Images - Position on right to balance left-aligned text

### ❌ Never do this:
- [Trying to exceed character limits makes text unreadable. Stick to 16 chars/line for headlines, 20 for subtext.]
- [Vague button text like "Click Here" underperforms. Use specific action verbs like "Shop Now" or "Learn More".]
- Place content outside safe zones
